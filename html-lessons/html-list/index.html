Swifty's Meatball recipe

Tried this a while ago and found it again so i thought to put it in here. (I just stole that recipe)

Ingredients for 4-8 servings

  • 1 lb ground beef (455 g)
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup bread crumbs (30 g)
  • 4 tablespoons Chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • 1 batch pizza dough
  • 1 cup marinara sauce (260 g), plus more for serving
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (100 g)
  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese

Recipe

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, 2 tablespoons of parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Pinch off a ball of the mixture, roll between your hands to make ping pong ball-sized meatball. Repeat with the rest of the meat.
  3. In a large pan, cook the meatballs over medium heat, turning to brown each side. Remove from heat and drain on a paper towel.
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F (180˚C).
  5. Pinch off a portion of pizza dough and, with your hands or a rolling pin, flatten out and top with a tablespoon of marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Add a meatball and wrap the dough over, pinching at the seams to close. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Arrange the balls in a circle, seam-side down, in a large cast-iron skillet.
  8. Mix the melted butter with the garlic, remaining 2 tablespoons of parsley, and the Parmesan. Brush the balls with the garlic butter mixture.
  9. Bake for 20 minutes, or until golden brown. Let cool for 5-10 minutes.
  10. Place a bowl of warm marinara sauce in the middle of the skillet, then serve.

Enjoy!
